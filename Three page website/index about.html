    Founded in Shanghai in 2014, Xiaoguo Culture is the most groundbreaking young comedy company in China. The company was founded by the behind-the-scenes team of the first domestic comedy talk show "Tonight's Post-80s Talk Show". It has continuously expanded its content track, and successively produced popular online dramas "Tucao Conference", and "Talk Show Conference" and offline experiential scene performances "Laughing Fruit Factory", comedy comprehensive show "Saturday Night Live" and many other strong young comedy IPs.

    Xiaoguo Culture has also created a series of offline performances, including Xiaoguo Talk Show, Mancai Special, Main Show, Talk Show City Roaming, etc. More performances will also go to all parts of the country to meet you, including Xiaoguo Talk Show City Roaming, and the main shows of talk show actors such as Xiaojia, Jiang Zihao, Zhang Jun, Qiu Rui, Jia Hua, etc. have also been staged all over the country.

    In addition, Xiaoguo Culture also has artists and screenwriters with their own characteristics, such as Li Dan, Chi Zi, Jianguo, Cheng Lu, Shi Yan, Siwen, Liang Haiyuan, and Qiong Xiaofeng. Driven by offline comedy talent training and the popularization of young comedy culture; on this basis, books, paid audio, theme spaces, training camps, comedy festivals and other products have been derived, gradually turning young comedy into a new lifestyle, conveying a positive, optimistic and humorous attitude towards life.
